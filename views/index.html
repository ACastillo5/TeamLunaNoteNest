About:

A student can make an account and upload notes for future students of the same course. These notes will be able to be searched by course number and professor. Additionally professors can make an account where they can view students' notes and take notes down if they violate terms, such as if they have test answers. A user will be able to view notes as well as upvote or downvote them, or report them to the professor.
